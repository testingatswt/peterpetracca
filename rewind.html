Rewind

It's easy to forget a year's worth of happenings. Arrivals fade into departures, one month blends into the next, and memories lose their linearity. Sure, we'll remember key events, but the timeline – the journey – becomes fuzzy. I wanted a way to preserve this.

Inspired by One Second Everyday, I spend the year taking short videos on a near-weekly basis. Shot entirely on an iPhone 6s, these clips chronologically tie together the highlights, shortcomings, and the routines, of my past two years.
