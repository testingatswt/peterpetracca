International Entrepreneurship

Moving and launching a startup in Vietnam has been an incredibly rewarding experience, though this did come with a steep learning curve – one that I've personally endured, and often struggled with. Building a local team, working with vendors, complying with local laws – these are all necessities in business, and different wherever you go.

Having traveled to 40 different countries for both work and pleasure, I've deeply enjoyed sharing my experiences, insights, and key learnings, to encourage others to bridge global communities and promote borderless entrepreneurship.
